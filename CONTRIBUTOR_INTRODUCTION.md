# Contributor Introductions

Welcome to the EcoPlay contributor community.

This space is for contributors to introduce themselves, share what they are interested in, and discover other people working around environmental education, games, community features, and open source.

## How to Add Your Introduction

1. Copy the template below.
2. Add your introduction under the **Introductions** section.
3. Keep your entry friendly, concise, and relevant to open-source collaboration.
4. Share only public contact links that you are comfortable making visible.
5. Submit your change through a focused pull request.

Please avoid posting private information, spam links, or unrelated promotion. The goal is to help contributors find teammates, reviewers, mentors, and people with similar interests.

## Introduction Template

```md
### Your Name

- GitHub: [@username](https://github.com/username)
- Location or timezone: City, country or timezone
- Interests: React, TypeScript, sustainability, game design, AI, documentation, testing
- Skills: A short list of technologies or areas you can help with
- Looking to collaborate on: Features, bug fixes, docs, testing, UI, or ideas
- Social links: LinkedIn, portfolio, X/Twitter, or other public links
- Fun eco fact or goal: One small sustainability habit or topic you care about
```
